About

My Story

Born and raised in Key West, Florida, a tropical island called home by many an author, musician, poet, pirate, treasure hunter, and many more fascinating characters, my culturally diverse and creative hometown cultivated a great passion for expanding my knowledge and comfort zone. I love some good old-fashioned rock n[']roll, and my two little dogs make me very happy. My passions include playing/listening to music, staying active, SCUBA diving, travel, history, art, and the animal refuge I co-founded in Nicaragua. Empathy, and helping others, have always been a big part of my life.

Isn[']t that what UX designers do, empathize?

I am a UX/UI designer with a background in behavioral analysis and real estate. My life experience has laid a foundation for the sound application of UX research and design principles. My wide range of cultural and business experience, including living in Germany and Mexico, has left me adept at communicating in multiple languages, cooperating readily with others regardless of any difficulties presented, and being able to solve problems with limited resources.
