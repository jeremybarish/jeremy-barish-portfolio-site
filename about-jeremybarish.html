About

My Story

I'm a Key West native, born and raised in a tropical paradise where authors, musicians, poets, pirates, and all sorts of interesting characters have called home. Growing up in this culturally diverse and creative environment sparked a real drive in me to never stop expanding my horizons.


Professionally, I wear the hat of an aspiring designer with a background in behavioral analysis and real estate. My life journey has set me up to apply UX research and design principles like a pro.


Personally, I'm all about good old-fashioned rock and roll, and let's not forget my two little doggies - they're my daily dose of happiness. My interests are all over the place, from playing guitar and listening to music, hitting the gym, SCUBA diving, exploring new places, or digging into history and art. I even co-founded an animal refuge in Nicaragua.


I have lived in Mexico and Germany, and it's given me a knack for languages and a real talent for working with others, no matter how tricky the situation. Problem-solving with limited resources? Been there, done that. So, that's a bit about me - a mix of a musician's charm, passion for life, and a professional journey that's taken me all over the place.
